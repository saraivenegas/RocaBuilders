Custom Home Building

Roca Builders adapts to fit your needs for new home construction. For homeowners already working with an architect, we will turn your vision into a reality. Our industry experience and passion for custom home building will ensure that no detail is overlooked when bringing your home plans to life. Whatever the situation, we want to build your new home and make construction of your new home easier for you. From laying the foundation to the finishing details, we will make sure that you are delighted with every stage of the process and your new home turns out just the way you expected it to look and feel.

Custom Home Design

Our most distinguished service for homeowners looking for a single source solution for designing and building their next home. This streamlined service combines award-winning architecture, detail-focused building, and dedicated project management. Benefits for homeowners include time and money savings. Because much of the back and forth between architect, builder, and homeowner is limited, we are able to exceed your expectations, not your budget. If you own a home or are looking to purchase property and you do not yet have a set of home plans, our design build service is likely the right solution for you.


Home Renovations
